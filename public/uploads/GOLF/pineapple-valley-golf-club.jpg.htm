Pineapple Valley Golf Club Hua Hin (Formerly Banyan Golf Club)

Pineapple Valley Golf Club Hua Hin (formerly Banyan Golf Club) opened in 2009 but despite its youth is leading the pack in Hua Hin. Built on the site of a former pineapple plantation, the golf course features many elevated greens, sloping fairways, meandering creeks, and well-placed bunkers. Pineapple Valley Golf Club has been awarded as "The Best New Course in the Asia Pacific" in 2009 by the readers of Asian Golf Monthly.

To enlarge the photo, please click on the thumbnail.

The golf course offers beautiful views of the mountains to the west, and ocean views to the east, of what is undoubtedly one of the best layouts in Hua Hin.

Pineapple Valley Golf Club features deep green Paspalum grassed tee boxes each with four tee distances that deliver a range of challenging play to suit every golfer’s ability. It is one of the most natural courses in Hua Hin, built around inherent lies and incorporating Pineapple Valley trees and rock formations into its well-designed holes.

The course has lush Zoysia grassed fairways, super fast greens and enough water to ensure you leave the occasional balls behind. The use of remnant pineapple groves in some rough areas makes for some challenging shots if you stray too far from the fairways.
